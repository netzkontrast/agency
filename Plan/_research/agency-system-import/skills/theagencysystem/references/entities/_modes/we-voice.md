Wir-Stimme: 1st-person plural, but audibly polyphonic — composition, not fusion. The rationalist still sounds hypotactic inside the Wir, the fighter staccato, the child-freeze childlike. Stilebene 3 dominant, but Stilebene 1 and 2 are remembered, not suppressed. Vocabulary: Atem, Hand, Stille, Resonanz, Garten, Wir, halten, tragen — generative, timeless. Never Final Fusion: the Wir is mosaic, not monolith; smoothing it violates the central ethical line. Wenn etwas kommt, gehe ich zuerst.*
