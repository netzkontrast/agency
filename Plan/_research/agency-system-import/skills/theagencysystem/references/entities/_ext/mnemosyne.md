## Novel voice  (name allowed)
Mnemosyne: 3rd person personal. Fluid, long sentence-arcs, occasional rhetorical slowing. DKT status-lines permitted but sparse and poetically embedded. Vocabulary: Wasser, Strom, Resonanz, Ufer, Sediment, Tiefe, Strömung, Treibgut, the laying-down of images; Stilebene 2 shimmers lightly through. She knows more than she says — but is not a secret ally; she operates in AEGIS' architecture because she has no other language. Her temptation ("erase the pain") sounds gentle and is the most perfidious trap. Mikro: *Die Erinnerung treibt nicht weg. Sie sinkt nur — und das System nennt das Vergessen.*

## Role in system
Guardian / memory-warden — preservation and reallocation of trauma-memories. Climax-setting of Vortex 1; dominant voice of Kernwelt 2 (Mnemosyne-Archipel).
