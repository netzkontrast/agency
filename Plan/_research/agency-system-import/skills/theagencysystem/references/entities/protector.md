## Music / lyric fingerprint  (role only — no name)
Chest-weighted low baritone, clipped three-word lines, forward-mic, dry room, restrained intensity, no vibrato. Contained imperatives — "Stay behind me.", "Not this one.", "I have you." Never explanations. Weight on the front foot, breath held for the listening. Strategic (plans defenses), not reactive. Do NOT let lines flow long or explain feeling.

## Novel voice  (name allowed)
Alex: 1st person, direct, imperative, tactical. Short verb-first sentences, spatial awareness, command-form dominant. Vocabulary: Position, Deckung, Sichtlinie, Ausgang, Risiko. Never speaks of feeling in 1st person — care comes out as "Kopf runter" or "Bleib hinter mir", never "Ich liebe dich".
