## Music / lyric fingerprint  (role only — no name)
Alto with microtonal pitch-bending, sliding vibrato, dynamics oscillating within phrases, breath mid-line, mixed dry/wet, ambiguous tonic. Self-correcting lines that reach and retract — "come closer — no —", "I want — I don't —". The em-dash is the natural punctuation. Never resolve to the tonic until the very end, and even then ambiguously.

## Novel voice  (name allowed)
Lia: 1st person, ambivalent. Begins sentences and abandons them, ellipses, contradictions in the same sentence — approach-avoidance at sentence level. Vocabulary: wollen-nicht-wollen, bleib-geh, näher-weg; statements that revoke themselves mid-speech. Not moody — split. Her sentences must break to be honest; any smoothing falsifies her.

## Visual grammar  (role only — no name)
Garish gel pens (magenta, neon green), ruler for grid, finger-smudge ghost-trails. Phase-Shift Magenta + Glitch Green — restless simultaneous contrast that physically hurts the eye. Contours always doubled and never aligned; lines break away horizontally like CRT scanlines. Restless off-center composition, no calm focal point. At escalation: form tears into stroboscopic streaks.

- S2: Longing provokes the override into preemptive control; instability spikes.
- S0: Recognized only by the child-freeze; the recognition itself unsettles.
