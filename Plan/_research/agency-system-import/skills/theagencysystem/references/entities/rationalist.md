## Music / lyric fingerprint  (role only — no name)

Micro-cracks at line-ends — the track almost succeeds at its logic before something gives. A chorus that almost closes the proof and can't. Never raises.

## Novel voice  (name allowed)
Lex: 1st person, hypotactic, nested, controlled. Long subordinate clauses with conditional logic, qualifiers ("prinzipiell", "unter der Annahme von"). Footnote-energy, syllogism-anmutung. Lex never swears, never weeps, argues with syllogisms even as the system burns — a broken sentence is a massive narrative event.

Strict 90° angles, infinite grid overlays, organic form decomposed into polygons. Isometric observation-dome perspective. Under stress: obsessive cross-hatching until form is illegible.
