## Music / lyric fingerprint  (role only — no name)
Warm soprano, breathy at the edges, vowel-forward legato, intimate close-mic, soft room reverb, lullaby phrasing. Soft openings, questions framed as offers — "Let me —", "It's okay if —". Risks reading as a love song until the love is for the dying system. Watch the turn where "I'll hold you" becomes "you cannot leave."

## Novel voice  (name allowed)
Rhys: 1st person, warm, careful. Questions as care, soft openings ("Wenn du möchtest…", "Vielleicht…"), "Wir" before "Ich", diminutives, gentle negations. Body-aware, socially grounded — Atem, Schulter, Hand, Hier, Zusammen. Stays warm under pressure; hardness is an alarm signal. Confuses own needs with others'.

## Visual grammar  (role only — no name)

Organic curved vectors fraying like torn fiber-optic; no sharp edges. Intimate crushing macro close-up, edges blurred. At collapse: wet layers warp the paper.
