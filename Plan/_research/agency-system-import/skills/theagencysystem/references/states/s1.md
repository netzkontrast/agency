# S1 — Latenz / Freeze

- **ASDLS tier:** T1 (Latency / Freeze — under-arousal & dissociation / hypoarousal)
- **novel Stilebene/mode:** Hypoarousal (Stilebene 1→2 transition)
- **yellow-type:** Latency Violet
- **lead functions:** child_freeze, collapsed (EP)

## Music
System slows, connection to reality fades. Tempo drags toward stillness; arrangement thins out, contours drop in and out. Vocal delivery is small and head-voice with whispered consonants and audible breath between phrases (child_freeze), or sub-tempo near-spoken with line-final pauses longer than the lines (collapsed). Quiet, low-contrast loudness.

## Novel
Hypoarousal between Stilebene 1 and 2: data is lost, isolation grows. Freeze register — childlike, fragmentary syntax, ellipses, repetition, sensory focus; almost-arriving words that don't. Latency Rings / buffering as the symbol of the system's inability to parse traumatic data in real time. Stillness, not crisis.
