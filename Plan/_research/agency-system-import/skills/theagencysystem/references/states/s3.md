# S3 — Kollaps-Peak

- **ASDLS tier:** T3 (Kernel Panic — system collapse)
- **novel Stilebene/mode:** Vortex 1 (maximal simultaneity; Stilebene 2 at peak overload)
- **yellow-type:** Flame / Corrupted (Flame Orange + Corrupted Yellow)
- **lead functions:** collapsed (peak) (EP)

## Music
Cognitive core fails; the reactive instinct-core takes over and emotional pain peaks. Either total over-saturation of energy or sub-tempo drone collapse — the floor of the album. Vocal delivery is deepest-in-the-body, gravelly, near-spoken, breath audible between heavy fragments that stay where they fell (collapsed at peak). Highest contrast; loudness either blown-out or crushed to a drone.

## Novel
Vortex 1: maximal simultaneity — the Wir-Stimme and AEGIS' macro-log audible at once; Beat 3 is event-less sensory silence, Beat 5 is AEGIS' voice extinguishing into algorithmic melancholy. Implosive circle-logic of shame, self-negation, inevitability; repetition as the rhythm of collapse. The novel itself writes slower under sustained presence.

## Visual / design  (hex · glitch · tier)
Flame Orange `#FF4500` and Corrupted Yellow `#8B8B00` over-saturate and bleed together; highest contrast. Glitch = extreme datamoshing, macro-blocking, torn geometries, uncontrolled noise, destruction of the interface grid. Tier 3 composition: claustrophobic macro close-up, frame blown apart by code-debris, total chaos. (Flame Orange must never meet Clean Ping — law of exclusion.)
