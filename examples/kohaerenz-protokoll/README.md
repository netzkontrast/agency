# Kohärenz Protokoll — canon corpus (worked example)

A six-document canon set for a single novel-in-progress, **Kohärenz
Protokoll** (German, hard-SF / philosophical-horror / psychological-thriller).
Ingested from Google Drive on 2026-06-10 via `research.ingest_gdoc` (Spec 126,
subagent-isolated — no document body ever entered the orchestrator context),
and preserved here as the worked example that **grounds the novel-capability
feature wave Specs 136–141**. Every spec in that wave cites a section of these
files as its `mvp-source`.

This is also a reference for what a *maximally demanding* novel project looks
like: a 41-chapter book whose structure runs **two simultaneous Dramatica
storyforms**, a **13-alter dissociative protagonist**, a strict
**reveal-discipline**, an author-authored **self-review rule-set**, and a
**provenance/lock** regime on every canon fact. The agency novel capability
was built (Specs 101–135) and then *extended* (136–141) against exactly this
material.

## What this novel needs that drove Specs 136–141

Reading the corpus reveals six structural capabilities the existing novel cap
(Specs 101–135) genuinely **cannot express** — each became a spec:

1. **Dual-storyform (Spec 136).** Two complete, simultaneous Dramatica
   storyforms (A = *Heuristics of Integration*, Kael-MC; B = *Phoenix
   Collapse*, AEGIS-MC) related by an involutive Klein-c inversion, with a
   **Vortex** where B is overtaken by A and then a post-orthodox synthesis.
   Dramatica — and the agency engine until 136 — knows only one storyform.
   *(File 01 §2, File 02 §9.)*

2. **Canon provenance & locks (Spec 137).** Every fact carries a marker —
   `[K]` canonical / `[V]` proposal / `[S]` quarry (deprecated-but-reusable) /
   `[L]` gap — plus dated **locks** with a source-hierarchy (newer wins) and a
   master-index checked before any contested decision. *(File 02 §16, File 05
   §12.)*

3. **Plural-character system (Spec 138).** The protagonist is not one person
   but a **system** of 13 alters (ANP/EP/special/mirror) with a phobia-driven
   conflict matrix, two trauma-layers, a switch mechanic, and the hard rule
   that alters are *never labeled in prose — only recognized by syntax +
   somatik + lexicon*. Resolution is functional multiplicity, **never
   fusion**. *(File 03, entire.)*

4. **Reveal-discipline (Spec 139).** "What may the reader know when, what may
   they NOT know, through what do they learn it?" — tracked across three
   audience horizons (reader / POV-character / antagonist), with a
   **multiplicity-veil** that holds until ~ch13, and deliberate Iser
   "Leerstellen" as first-class features. *(File 05 §6, File 02 §13.)*

5. **Project rule-sets & motif discipline (Spec 140).** The KP's own
   per-scene **self-review rule-set** (R-1…R-N with Critical/Medium/Low defect
   tiers), the **hot-polarity rule** (cold ozone = AEGIS ≠ warmth = Juna,
   never mixed), the **max-1-Genesis-echo-per-scene** budget, and tracked
   **foreshadowing anchors** (the number 734, the Telefon-Stille, the Silas
   half-sentence). *(File 05 §10, §4, §5, §2.1.)*

6. **Chapter briefing & mode-blocks (Spec 141).** The 13-section chapter
   briefing (the bridge between storyform-encoding and prose-drafting), the
   five+ **narrative-mode blocks** (each carrying mode / storyform-status /
   bridge-frequency), the crucial **mode-change ≠ storyform-boundary**
   distinction, and the **genre-per-act** discipline. *(File 05 §9, §11;
   File 01 §1.)*

## Three things to carry away

1. *The novel is an ontological healing-loop.* Beginning and end coincide, but
   the seeing has changed — the structure (Genesis-bracket Kap 0 ↔ Kap 40,
   Ouroboros Kap 1 ↔ Kap 39) IS the meaning.
2. *The dual-storyform is the form of the separation in narrative form* — and
   its healing is the re-joining in a third, plural form. That is why Spec 136
   models it as a `StoryformSet` whose Klein-c inversion check proves the two
   forms are *the two halves of one severed whole*.
3. *Voices are recognized, never named.* The ethical line of the novel —
   no alter blurs into another, none is eliminated, none becomes a caricature
   — is what Spec 138's `check_alter_recognition` + anti-cliché taboo-rules
   make machine-checkable.

## Provenance convention (used throughout the corpus)

`[K]` kanonisch/gelockt · `[V]` Vorschlag (proposal, validation-pending) ·
`[S]` Steinbruch (quarry — deprecated material, reusable as raw stock) ·
`[L]` Lücke (gap, still to set). **Never silently canonize speculation.**
Spec 137 makes this convention a first-class graph surface.
